# Python in Economics and Business Analytics

## Introduction
Python is a simple and beginner-friendly programming language. It is widely used in economics, finance, and business analytics for working with data, building models, and automating tasks.

---

## History and Background
- Created by Guido van Rossum in 1991
- Designed to be readable and easy to learn
- Popular in research, statistics, and analytics
- Now widely used in finance and consulting industries

---

## Key Concepts
- Variables
- Data types
- Lists and dictionaries
- Conditions
- Loops
- Functions
- Libraries like NumPy and Pandas

---

- Readable syntax

- Useful for research and business
- Saves time through automation
