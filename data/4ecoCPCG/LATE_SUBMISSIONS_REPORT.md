## 💡 Insights

### Why Late Performance is Lower

Possible factors:
1. **Students who missed deadline** may have:
   - Incomplete implementations
   - Didn't have time to test thoroughly
   - Conceptual misunderstanding

2. **Sample skew**: Only 15 students vs 26 on-time
   - Smaller sample has more variability
   - Extreme poor performers pull down average

3. **Different students**: Some on-time perfect scorers didn't submit late
   - Results not comparable 1-to-1

### Positive Note

- **11/15 (73%) still got perfect scores**
- Even late, most students demonstrated mastery
- Issues concentrated in small subset
